Hello we are
Outliers!

Founded by a group of students at MIT & Harvard, Outliers was a special interest group for radical research, venture and investment opportunities. In 2016, we crowdfunded $2M to invest in 32 crypto projects. 2017, we read 880+ papers collectively. 2018, we exited the crypto fund at $32.4M and dropped out from schools. 2019, we accelerated the growth, adoption, and impact for 9 startups. With the research foundations & startup experiences, we decided to launch two new initiatives in 2020: Outliers Fellowship & Space Fund.
