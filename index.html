Outliers Fund is a collective of

research-driven funds & initiatives

Founded by students from MIT & Harvard, #OUTLIERS started as a special interest group on Slack for radical research & venture discussions. We raised $2M for 32 crypto projects (Crypto Fund I) in 2016, launched an algorithmic trading system (Quant Fund I) in 2017, exited the Crypto Fund at $32.4M in 2018, and accelerated the #growth #adoption #impact for 9 startups (Accelerator Batch I) in 2019. With these research foundations & startup experiences, we evolved and became a collective of research-driven funds (Outliers Fund). Today we study, invest and accelerate "outliers", those who dare to take the road less treveled and change the lives of the majority!
