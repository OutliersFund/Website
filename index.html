Outliers Fund is a collective of

research-driven funds, accelerator & fellowship

#OUTLIERS was originally a special interest group (SIG) for SciFi-inspired research by students from MIT & Harvard. We read SciFi novels, watched SciFi movies, ﻿and discussed what could be imagineered in reality and published scientific papers from these curiosity-based inspirations and explorations. As a community, we syndicated $2M in 2016 (Outliers Fund I) / studied 880+ papers & invested in 32 projects in 2017 / exited Fund I at $32M+ in 2018 / accelerated 9 startups (Outliers Accelerator) and invested in 6 of them (Outliers Fund II) in 2019 / 1 startup became unicorn and 2 filing IPO in 2020 / exited Fund II at $23M and raising $50M (Outliers Fund III) in 2021. With these research foundations & venture experiences, the original student-run SIG evolved to become Outliers Fund, a research-driven venture fund/accelerator for #blockchain #space #energy. We bet on ideas that turn science fiction into scientific facts and outliers who push the boundaries of science and imaginations.
